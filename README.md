## 👋 About Me

I'm an Agricultural Systems Engineer and Graduate Research Assistant at the **University of Nebraska-Lincoln**, building data-driven tools to make farming smarter, more sustainable, and more resilient.

My work sits at the intersection of **machine learning**, **IoT sensor systems**, and **geospatial analysis** — translating raw field data into decisions that matter for producers, livestock managers, and land stewards.
